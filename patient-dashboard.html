My visits

Track requests you’ve sent in. Your care team reviews each visit when it’s your turn.

What the statuses mean
In review — in line for a clinician Completed — you’ve received a response or plan
What happens next
  1. Your answers are reviewed by a licensed clinician (not by the chat assistant alone).
  2. This service is asynchronous — there is no live video visit in this flow.
  3. If your case is routine, you’ll typically hear back within about two hours (demo timings below).
  4. For emergencies, use emergency services — this platform isn’t for life-threatening problems.

Sample visits load from a file in this folder. If you completed “Start a visit” on this device, your most recent summary may appear at the top. Nothing is saved to a remote database.
